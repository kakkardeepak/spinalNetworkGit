Rehabilitation

Our members recognise the challenges facing those with SCI and other neurological conditions. They are expert in identifying the barriers to independence and the impact and limitations that these have on an individual’s daily living. We design and provide bespoke occupational therapy rehabilitation programmes to overcome those barriers in many specialist fields. We undertake thorough assessment and provide reports to suit each individual and need. Our treatment outcome can be measured against goals and other clinical measures.

Housing

We strive to ensure that your home remains your home. We have a complete understanding of the housing adaptation needs for long term neurological conditions. We make recommendations for adaptations to make the environment(s) safe, accessible and enable an individual to function at their optimum level of independence. We are also experienced at working alongside architects and surveyors reviewing plans for adaptations.

Equipment

Our members are experts in a range of specialist equipment designed to optimise functional independence of our clients. This includes equipment for personal care, domestic and leisure activities. Some of our members are qualified to undertake a full risk assessment and make recommendations for manual handling equipment. We have links to representatives from a variety of companies to arrange trials of equipment

Transport & Vehicles

Expertise to assess and recommend suitable cars and vehicles for those with spinal cord injury and other neurological conditions is embedded in the skill set of our members. Including for those who need to drive from or travel from their wheelchair. We have links to a variety of companies offering adaptations and conversions to a range of vehicles to suit each individual's need.

Functional Rehabilitation

Our network members can work with individuals to provide a rehabilitation programme to help restore or optimise independence in daily living tasks. Strategies or techniques can be taught along with practising of functional tasks or introducing small aids to promote independence. The approach is always client and goal led in the areas prioritised by clients.

Upper limb & Hand Rehabilitation

Our network members are experienced in a range of ‘hands-on’ techniques such as Bobath and PNF that can promote rehabilitation and maximise therapy outcomes. They are also experienced in the use of specialist technologies such as functional electrical stimulation (FES), robotics and virtual reality, evidenced to improve clinical outcomes. After a thorough assessment, a bespoke training programme will be drawn up incorporating the use of specialist technologies where appropriate. We also offer training to the carers to carry out a home programme.

Seating & Posture

Our network members are experienced in the management of complex postural needs as a result of neurological injury. With the knowledge of a wide range of seating products and years of experience in the field, clients can be assisted to identify a suitable wheelchair and postural supports. After an in depth assessment a bespoke seating and mobility solution can be recommended as per the clients aspiration, environment and their clinical and functional needs. Our network has links with various suppliers of specialist seating and wheelchairs. We can arrange trials of the recommended products.
