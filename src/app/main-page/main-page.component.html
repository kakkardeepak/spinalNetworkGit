I have highly specialist skills in the field of spinal cord injury with 18 years of experience in the specialist area. I am expert in occupational therapy assessment and intervention including wheelchair and seating assessments, hand therapy, functional rehabilitation and prescription of equipment and home adaptations. I hold Level 4 Advanced People Handling and Risk Assessment Key Trainer’s Certificate and can undertake risk assessments for safe manual handling.
